About Capture

Capture is developed by an enthusiastic team dedicated to transparency and authenticity in digital content. We believe that truth is not simply black-and-white but a continuum of interconnected stories. Our vision is to collaborate with individuals and companies to ensure credible sources of digital content with transparent and immutable records, enabling free debate, narration, and analysis on the internet.

Our Mission

To restore trust in digital content by providing robust provenance solutions, we aim to empower publishers, creators, and consumers by offering tools that guarantee the authenticity, integrity, and transparency of digital media.

Global Team

With team members based around the world, especially in London, LA, Dubai and Asia, Numbers brings a truly global perspective to media and asset management, allowing us to cater to diverse market needs effectively.  

Eco-Friendly

Numbers is committed to sustainable practices by using low-emission blockchains, minimizing our carbon footprint and setting an example for responsible tech innovation.  

Security & Authenticity

Numbers is ISO27001 (Information Security Standard) certified and an early member of C2PA (Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity). We prioritize trust and safeguard your digital assets with the highest standards.  

50/50 Gender Split

Our diverse team represents a balanced perspective, fostering an inclusive work environment that leads to more comprehensive solutions.

Safeguarding the world's most important media

Numbers has proven its robustness and reliability by working in challenging environments like war zones and during elections, helping to protect and record crucial global events.

Community Driven

As a decentralized network, Numbers emphasizes a community-driven approach, actively engaging in discussions. We've initiated Ethereum asset standards like ERC7053 and ERC7517 to ensure transparency, integrity, and best practices in digital asset management.
