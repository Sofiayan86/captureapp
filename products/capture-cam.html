FAQs

What is Capture Cam?
Capture Cam is a C2PA-compliant blockchain camera that captures photos with secure provenance, timestamp, geolocation, and copyright records.
How does Capture Cam ensure authenticity?
Capture Cam ensures authenticity by securely recording the provenance, timestamp, geolocation, and copyright information of each photo and video.
Can I use Capture Cam for professional purposes?
Yes, Capture Cam is designed for both professionals and enthusiasts who prioritize authenticity, ownership, and copyright protection.
How can I integrate Capture SDK into my platform?
You can integrate the Capture SDK into your platform by following the documentation provided on our website.
What is the Capture Eye widget?
The Capture Eye widget is a content provenance widget that can be integrated into your website to display the provenance and copyright information of your content.
